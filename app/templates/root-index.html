Frequent Questions

  • What is a "Crew?"

    A Crew is what we call your group of people staying connected. They can be family, friends, co-workers - anyone you want to include in your messaging group.

  • Can I choose my Crew's Account PIN?

    The Account PIN is a unique idenitfier that your Crew members must enter to gain access to the account. No, you cannot choose the Account PIN, it is automatically assigned when your account is created.

  • Do all my Crew members need to register for an account?

    No, a Crew only requires one person to register - they will be the account admin. Crew members can access the account message center via any phone by just knowing the dial-in number and Account PIN.

  • Are Messages encrypted?

    No, Messages are not encrypted. {{ config.app_name }} is not intended to be a secure communication service, it only serves as an easily-accessible hub for a small group of people to communicate with voice memos. While your Messages are not publicly-available, you should not treat this as a secure platform.

  • Is there any way to "protect" an account?

    Yes. Your Crew admin can add an Access Code to the account. This is an additional layer of protection that must be input before a Crew member can access the account. The Access Code can be chosen by the admin.
